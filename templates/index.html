WEB APPLICATION TO DETERMINE PERCENTAGE OF PHARMACEUTICAL SPENDING

The aim of our app is to explore pharmaceutical spending data across the globe, to determine which countries have the highest spending percentage on off the shelf pharmaceutical medicines.

This information can be beneficial for pharmaceutical companies to promote products globally.
